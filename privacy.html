Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 3, 2026

Graffiti Spider Cards is a card game with local progress saving and advertising-supported hint access.

1. Information We Collect

The app does not require an account and does not request your name, email address, contacts, photos, or payment information. If you allow App Tracking Transparency permission, the app may access the device advertising identifier to support advertising, ad measurement, and fraud prevention. Advertising SDKs may process device, ad interaction, and diagnostic information according to their own privacy practices.

2. Local Game Data

The app may store gameplay progress, settings, unlocked levels, best times, move counts, music preferences, and sound preferences locally on your device. This data stays on your device and is used only to provide the game experience.

3. Network Access

Core gameplay, levels, local progress, music, and sound effects can work without an account. Internet access may be used to request advertising placement configuration and to load rewarded ads, including when you request a hint.

4. Third-Party Services

Graffiti Spider Cards integrates advertising services, including Chartboost, Yandex Mobile Ads, Vungle, and InMobi. The app does not integrate social login, cloud sync, or in-app payment services.

5. Apple Diagnostics

If you choose to share diagnostics with Apple, Apple may provide developers with aggregated crash or performance information through Apple developer tools. We do not use this information to identify you or track you.

6. Children

Graffiti Spider Cards does not knowingly collect personal information from children. The app contains no gambling, cash wagering, or user-generated content.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Advertising partners may process information as needed to load, deliver, limit, measure, and protect ads.

8. Data Deletion

You can remove locally stored game data by deleting the app from your device. If the app includes a reset progress option, using that option removes local progress within the app.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy if the app changes. Any update will be reflected by changing the effective date above.

10. Contact

For privacy questions, use the developer contact information listed on the Graffiti Spider Cards App Store product page.
